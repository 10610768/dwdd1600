Our History

At Cloud Nine Destinations, we're passionate about helping you find your perfect beach escape. Since our founding in 2015, we've been dedicated to providing unforgettable seaside experiences. Our expertise in curating exceptional beach vacations has made us a trusted name in the travel industry.

Founded by beach lovers, Cloud Nine Destinations started with a dream to share the beauty of the world's most stunning coastlines. With years of experience, we've become experts in creating personalized beach getaways that cater to all types of travelers.

Our Clients

We serve a diverse range of clients, from honeymooners seeking romantic beachfront retreats to families looking for fun in the sun, and solo travelers in search of relaxation by the waves. No matter what kind of beach vacation you're dreaming of, Cloud Nine Destinations is here to make it happen.

Popular Beach Destinations

  • Corfu, Greece: Explore the crystal-clear waters and rich history of this Ionian island.
  • Islamorada, Florida: Enjoy the laid-back charm and vibrant marine life in the heart of the Florida Keys.
  • Myrtle Beach, South Carolina: Experience fun-filled family vacations with beautiful sandy beaches and lively boardwalks.
  • Palawan Island, Philippines: Unwind on the stunning, unspoiled beaches and marvel at the incredible biodiversity.
  • Patong, Thailand: Dive into the energetic nightlife and pristine beaches of this popular destination.
  • Sayulita, Mexico: Relax in this bohemian paradise known for its surfing waves and vibrant local culture.
